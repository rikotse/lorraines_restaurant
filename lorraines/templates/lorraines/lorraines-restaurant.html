Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lorraine's has become Johannesburg's premier destination for fine dining. Chef Lorraine Chauke combines traditional South African ingredients with contemporary techniques to create unforgettable culinary experiences.

Our restaurant is located in the vibrant Sandton district, featuring an elegant interior with panoramic views of the city skyline.
